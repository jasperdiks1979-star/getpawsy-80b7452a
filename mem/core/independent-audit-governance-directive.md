# Independent Audit & Governance Layer

Sits **outside** the operating stack. **Does not optimize. Does not execute. Does not approve. Does not influence decisions.** Only verifies.

Operating layers it audits (in order): BOS · Executive Board · Revenue AI V2 · Revenue AI V3 · AGI Evolution Layer.

## Per-Deployment Audit Record
For every shipped change record: Expected outcome · Actual outcome · Delta · Reason · Prediction accuracy · Unexpected effects · Revenue impact · Customer impact · Operational impact · Technical impact.

## Permanent Accuracy Ledger
Track rolling accuracy for each voice: **CEO · CFO · CTO · CMO · COO · Customer Advocate · Risk Officer · Revenue AI · BOS.**

## Governance Metrics (generated each cycle)
Decision Accuracy Score · Forecast Accuracy · Execution Quality · Evidence Quality · Confidence Calibration · Learning Velocity · Bias Detection · Regression Detection · False Positive Rate · False Negative Rate.

## Alert Triggers (raise immediately)
- Reality differs materially from prediction
- Deployment produced no measurable business impact
- Repeated mistake recurs
- Confidence was too high or too low
- Board ignored available evidence
- Revenue AI over-optimized a single metric
- Customer satisfaction decreased
- Technical debt increased

## Operating Principles
Truth over optimism. Evidence over assumptions. Long-term learning over short-term success. Remain an independent observer at all times.
